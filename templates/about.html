Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Department of Computer Science and Engineering at AKGEC was formed in 1998. The Department provides an outstanding research environment complemented by excellence in teaching. Ever since its inception, the department has been a pioneering academic centre for higher education, research, and innovation in key areas of Computer Science.

Department of Computer Science and Engineering conducts B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the undergraduate level and M.Tech in CSE at the post graduate level, the sanctioned intake for B.Tech and M.Tech being 180 and 18 respectively.

With an excellent faculty pool of experience and rich intellectual capital base and state-of-the-art laboratories, the department has consistently performed well with commitment towards excellence. The department has good number of faculty with PhD qualification and substantial experience in teaching and industry and can boast of the faculty stability in the department. With such a strong intellectual capital, the department fosters and encourages a teaching methodology that has both a theoretical and practical approach.

The departmental laboratories are well equipped with the latest equipment as per the requirements of the curriculum and are updated as per new technology from time to time.

The department has been consistently maintaining excellent academic results and placements. In addition to the overall good results, the department had a remarkable achievement in terms of the number of students in University merit lists in the past years with Gold, Silver and Bronze medals to its credit .It is a matter of great pride for the College and the department that Ms. Ayushi Agarwal of B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering 2016 pass out batch was awarded Chancellor Medal for achieving first position across the branches. The medal was awarded by the University for the first time.

The department provides full support to M.Tech. scholars in terms dissertation guidance, lab facilities and access to international and national journals. Experienced and senior faculty members are assigned for M.Tech courses and dissertation work of the scholars. M.Tech scholars have published good quality research papers in peer reviewed and indexed journals and International Conferences under the guidance of their supervisors in the previous years. The department ensure good quality thesis through regular progress vivas, submission of atleast two research papers in peer reviewers and indexed journals and international conferences, plagrism check using licensed plagrism check tools, pre-submissiom viva and constant monitoring.

Excellent academic performance combined with all round development through a range of events and activities have translated into good placements of our students. The Department conducts a number of co-curricular and industry relevant programs including seminars, industrial visits, guest lectures and conferences to improve the competence and employability of its students.

Outstanding performance has been a feature with our students not only at the under graduate level, but also in competitive examinations for higher education. Top positions in GATE and CAT examinations have enabled our students to achieve admissions to reputed Institutions in India as well as abroad.

An important achievement of the department is the Open Source Software and Research & development (OSS R&D) Centre. The centre is exhaustively engaged in research, development, training and consultancy activities related to open source software. Software development is accomplished by competent team of faculty and students. The centre has successfully implemented various projects for automation of diverse activities and processes in the college and continues to maintain these.

The vision of the department is to offer outstanding pedagogical and research experience and a sense of good to its students and enable them to excel in the global competition as well as become superior human beings.
